Seoul Fashion Week

2022 shows

Street Style Day

Despite the surge of Covid cases, Seoul Fashion Week trudged on ahead. The opening highlight involved Lee Jung-Jae, who was chosen as the global ambassador of the event. A series of hybrid collections of both digital and physical shows were presented, inviting stylish attendees to visit the city’s Seoul Museum of Craft Art—the main venue for the season.
