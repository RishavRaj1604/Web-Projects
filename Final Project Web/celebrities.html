Sneak Peek in Celebrities life.

Jeon Jungkook on growing up in the public eye:

“I want to decide for myself how to live my life”

“There are lots of things I still want to do. I want to continue expanding as an artist and achieve even greater things”

V on passion and profession :

“Some say that you should keep your first love as a hobby, and not turn it into your job. I disagree”

“I don’t think change is a bad thing because I have learnt how to decide what is right for me and what isn’t”

BTS's Jimin on the group's next steps:

“Should we find something new, or should we refine what we’ve done?”

“These days, I’m more than just fine. I feel I’m allowed to be happy”

BTS's Jin on rest, the importance of uselessness and his unique philosophy of fun:

“You need to have days that other people consider wasteful, to be able to focus more on useful things later”

“I think having fun is one of the things that makes it possible for me to work without burning out”

BTS's j-hope on regaining confidence, staying positive and picking himself up after a slump:

“I think I came to like myself a little more and was able to grow as a person while working on the Love Yourself album”

“I tend to be heavily influenced by the people around me, and their warmth and sincerity have guided me to where I am today”

BTS's RM on the concept of eternity:

"I want to produce something long-lasting and timeless”

“My life may come with responsibilities. But this kind of life can be fun, and I intend to keep on living with this attitude

SUGA on the little things that matter:

"I try to find happiness in simple things, such as getting up early in the morning and having decaf coffee”

“If you obsess about it, you end up worrying too much. I think it’s perfectly fine to go with the flow, rather than constantly striving and struggling all the time”
