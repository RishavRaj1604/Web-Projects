Niacinamide- Skin Care

Niacinamide, also known as nicotinamide, is a form of Vitamin B3 that is water-soluble, which means that it is not stored in the body and is important to replenish. Niacinamides nourish while calming redness and inflammation. It has similar effects by strengthening the skin barrier, however it fortifies from the get go without sensitivity or irritation. It prevents transepidermal water loss and actually boosts the ability of other moisturizing ingredients to do so as well
